Beyond directories and guest posts, here's what will compound over time:

- **Hamburg Welcome Center** — content about digital services for new businesses in Hamburg
- **Local co-working spaces (betahaus Hamburg, Mindspace)** — events, workshops, mutual linking

### 2. Case Study Seeding

You have strong case studies (Blitz Hamburg ROI in 7 weeks, GL Sommer 728 actions/month). Turn these into:
- LinkedIn articles (linking to webseite.hamburg)
- Medium posts (dofollow from articles)

### 3. Tool/Resource Link Bait

Build one free mini-tool and launch it:
- "PageSpeed Check fuer Hamburger Unternehmen" — branded free tool
